About Covid-19

What is COVID-19 (Corona-Virus)

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). The virus is transmitted through direct contact with respiratory droplets of an infected person.

What are the Main Symptoms?

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways.
Most infected people will develop mild to moderate
illness and recover without hospitalization.

  • Fever
  • Dry Cough
  • Tiredness
  • Difficulty in breathing
  • Chest pain
  • Loss of speach or Movement
